About Us

The All India Inclusive Federation of Sports (AIIFS) stands as India's premier organization dedicated to revolutionizing sports development across all communities. Recognized by the Ministry of MSME, Government of India, AIIFS works tirelessly to discover, nurture, and promote athletic talent from every corner of our nation.AIIFS takes pride in our role as an enabler of dreams, helping athletes transform their sporting potential into achievements at state, national, and international levels. Our inclusive approach ensures that athletes with disabilities and those from underrepresented communities receive the same world-class training and opportunities.
